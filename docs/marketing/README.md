# Marketing Accuracy Status

> **Critical Issues Identified & Solutions Ready**
> Status: Analysis Complete | Fixes Ready | Timeline: 2-3 hours

---

## ⚠️ Executive Summary

**Issue**: Marketing materials describe features that are not implemented in the current codebase, creating customer expectation gaps.

**Impact**: HIGH - Customers may sign up expecting features that don't exist, leading to disappointment and potential churn.

**Status**: ✅ Analysis complete, 🛠️ Fixes ready, ⏱️ 2-3 hours to implement

**Risk Level**: P0 - Critical (needs immediate attention)

---

## 🎯 Problem Overview

### **Root Cause**
During rapid development, marketing copy was written based on planned features rather than implemented features. The disconnect occurred between:

1. **Product Vision**: What was planned and marketed
2. **Implementation Reality**: What was actually coded
3. **Documentation Gap**: No verification process between marketing and development

### **Affected Areas**
- **Website marketing pages**
- **Feature descriptions**
- **User workflow documentation**
- **Sales materials**

### **Customer Impact**
- **Expectation Mismatch**: Users expect collaborative features that don't exist
- **Trust Issues**: May appear misleading even if unintentional
- **Support Burden**: Support team handles disappointed customers
- **Churn Risk**: Users may leave due to unmet expectations
