## layout: default

# Executive Summary

This comprehensive learning blueprint addresses the critical skills gap in AI-powered social media marketing for 1000+ learners across Testslate's global technology workforce. Designed for social media managers, community managers, and early-career marketing professionals, this 12-week intensive program combines 80% hands-on application with 20% theoretical foundation across AI fundamentals, campaign optimization, content strategy, and automation workflows.

---

---

## layout: default

# Executive Summary (continued)

With a budget of ₹10,00,000 INR (approximately $12,000 USD), the program leverages industry-standard tools including ChatGPT for content generation, Midjourney for visual creation, Hootsuite for analytics, Meta Advantage+ for advertising, and Jasper for copywriting. The curriculum is structured around real-world scenarios including product launches, brand awareness campaigns, lead generation, crisis management, and influencer collaborations, culminating in a comprehensive capstone portfolio project.

---

---

## layout: default

# Executive Summary (continued)

Delivered through a hybrid model combining self-paced modules with collaborative workshops, the program targets 90% completion rate within 90 days, with daily engagement touchpoints and continuous assessment through knowledge checks and portfolio milestones. Success will be measured through completion rates, job performance improvements, and certification achievements, with quarterly content refreshes using agile sprint methodology to maintain relevance in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

## layout: table

# Risk Mitigation (1/3)

<div class="risk-table">

| Risk                                                        | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy                                                                                                                                                         |
| ----------------------------------------------------------- | ----------- | ------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Low learner engagement due to 20+ hour/week time commitment | Medium      | High   | Flexible pacing with 90-day completion window, micro-learning chunks \(15-20 min\), mobile-responsive design for commute learning, incentive program with milestone rewards |
| AI tool access limitations or cost overruns                 | Medium      | Medium | Negotiate educational pricing with vendors, provide shared team accounts, include free alternatives \(Claude, Bing Image Creator\), budget 10% contingency for tool costs   |
| Rapid AI tool evolution making content outdated             | High        | Medium | Modular content design for easy updates, quarterly refresh cycles, focus on principles over specific features, community-sourced updates from learners                      |
| Technical issues with LMS or authoring tools                | Low         | High   | Comprehensive testing before launch, backup hosting solutions, 24/7 technical support contact, offline downloadable content for critical modules                            |

</div>

# Sustainability Plan

<div class="glass-card p-6">

Long-term sustainability is ensured through a comprehensive maintenance and evolution strategy that treats this program as a living curriculum. Given the rapid pace of AI tool development, the program employs agile sprint methodology with quarterly content refreshes triggered by major tool updates, new platform features, or significant industry shifts. A dedicated community of practice comprising program alumni serves as both a support network and a crowdsourced intelligence system, identifying emerging trends and sharing real-world applications that inform curriculum updates.

The program's modular architecture enables surgical updates without full redesigns—individual modules can be refreshed independently while maintaining overall program coherence. A content governance committee meets monthly to review analytics, learner feedback, and industry developments, prioritizing updates based on impact and urgency. All content is version-controlled with clear update logs, ensuring transparency and enabling rollback if needed.

Scaling considerations include developing train-the-trainer programs to build internal facilitation capacity, creating localized versions for regional markets \(starting with Hindi and Southeast Asian languages\), and designing advanced cohorts for alumni seeking deeper specialization. The program's success metrics and ROI data will inform expansion decisions, with potential pathways including executive versions for leadership, specialized tracks for specific industries \(e-commerce, B2B, healthcare\), and integration into onboarding programs for new marketing hires.

Financial sustainability is achieved through a blended funding model: initial corporate investment covers development and Year 1 operations, while Years 2+ are supported by a combination of per-learner fees \(₹5,000 per participant\), efficiency gains from automation \(reduced external training costs\), and demonstrated ROI from improved campaign performance. A 3-year roadmap includes expansion to 3,000+ learners annually, development of 5 specialized tracks, and establishment of Testslate as a recognized leader in AI-powered marketing education within the technology sector.

## Maintenance Schedule

- **Review Frequency:** Quarterly content audits with monthly governance meetings

**Update Triggers:**

- Major AI tool updates \(e.g., GPT-5 release, significant Midjourney version changes\)
- New platform features from Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok requiring curriculum integration
- Learner feedback themes indicating content gaps or confusion \(3+ similar comments\)
- Industry best practice shifts identified through SME monitoring
- Regulatory changes affecting AI use in marketing \(e.g., disclosure requirements\)
- Completion rates dropping below 80% for specific modules
- Assessment performance declining below 75% pass rate
- Post-completion surveys indicating skill gaps or outdated content
